About

Providing affordable and professional painting, decorating and plastering services in the South East. We are a Kent County Council Trading Standards Approved Company and a proud member of Checkatrade. At South East Interiors and Exteriors we are focused on providing a high-quality professional decorating service, aimed at customer satisfaction - we will do everything we can to meet your expectations. We offer a wide range of interior and exterior services including:

  • Painting
  • Decorating
  • Spraying
  • Wallpapering
  • Wood staining
  • Plastering
  • Rendering
  • Drylining
  • Artexing
  • Stud wall erection
  • Property maintenance services
  • Guttering repairs
  • Full guttering replacement
  • Tiling services

With a variety of Decorating and Plastering Services to choose from, we're sure you'll be happy working with us. Look around our website and if you have any comments or questions, please feel free to contact us on 07860681642
